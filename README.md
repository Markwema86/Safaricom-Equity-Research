## Overview

This repository contains a comprehensive equity research report and detailed financial analysis of **Safaricom PLC**, one of East Africa's leading telecommunications companies. The project includes in-depth financial modeling, valuation analysis, and investment insights for Safaricom's stock.

## 📋 Project Contents

### Files Included

1. **Safaricom_Equity_Research_Report.docx**
   - Complete equity research report with detailed analysis
   - Company overview, business model, and market position
   - Financial performance analysis and projections
   - Investment thesis and valuation summary
   - Risk factors and investment recommendations

2. **Safaricom Financial Analysis (Autosaved).xlsx**
   - Comprehensive financial modeling spreadsheet
   - Historical financial statements (Income Statement, Balance Sheet, Cash Flow)
   - Financial ratios and key performance indicators (KPIs)
   - Valuation models (DCF, Comparable Companies Analysis, Precedent Transactions)
   - Sensitivity analysis and scenario modeling
   - Projection models for future financial performance

## 📈 About Safaricom PLC

**Safaricom** is the largest mobile network operator in East Africa, headquartered in Kenya. Key points:
- Market leader in Kenya with significant presence in other East African markets
- Primary revenue streams: Mobile services, data services, financial services
- Listed on the Nairobi Securities Exchange (NSE) and London Stock Exchange (LSE)
- Important player in Kenya's digital economy
